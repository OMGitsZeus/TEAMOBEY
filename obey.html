About TEAM OBEY

TEAM OBEY is a FiveM server dedicated to aviation enthusiasts and freeroam adventurers. Whether you're piloting a commercial jet, a fighter plane, or exploring the open world, TEAM OBEY offers a unique experience for all players.

Our server features a mix of freeroam, military operations, car meets, airshows, and light RP. Join us for an unforgettable experience in the skies and on the ground!
